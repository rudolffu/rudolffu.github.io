Some useful books for astronomers

  • Sep 13, 2018
  • One minute read

Textbooks or lecture notes

Astronomy, Astrophysics and Cosmology

  • Textbook on spherical astronomy, by W. M. Smart, and R. M. Green. Cambridge University Press, 1977.

Technical books or manuals

(Astro)Statistics, Data Mining, Machine Learning and AI

  • Introduction to Statistical Machine Learning, by Masashi Sugiyama. Elsevier Inc., 2016.

    • 中文版：统计机器学习导论 ，杉山将（著）；肖竹，谢宁，李柏杨（译），机械工业出版社，2018.

  • Data Science at the Command Line, by Jeroen Janssens. O’Reilly Media, Inc., 2014/2021

    • 中文版：命令行中的数据科学，Jeroen Janssens（著）；王晓伟，刘峰（译），人民邮电出版社，2015.

Python, R and other languages

  • Introducing Python: modern computing in simple packages, by Bill Lubanovic. O’Reilly Media, Inc., 2014.

    • 中文版：Python语言及其应用 ，Bill Lubanovic (著) ；梁杰 , 丁嘉瑞 , 禹常隆 (译)，人民邮电出版社，2016.

  • Python Data Science Handbook: Essential Tools for Working with Data, by VanderPlas, Jake. O’Reilly Media, Inc., 2016.

Databases, SQL and so on

  • High Performance MySQL, by Baron Schwartz, Peter Zaitsev, Vadim Tkachenko. O’Reilly Media, Inc., 2012.
    • 中文版：高性能MySQL ，Baron Schwartz, Peter Zaitsev, Vadim Tkachenko（著）；: 宁海元，周振兴，彭立勋，翟卫祥，刘辉(译)，电子工业出版社，2013.
